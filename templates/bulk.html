📋 How Bulk Predict Works

Upload a CSV with one customer per row. The model reads each row, scores it, and returns:

  • Churn probability (0–100%)
  • Risk level (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
  • Prediction label (Will Churn / Will Stay)
  • Top contributing feature

All results are saved to your prediction history automatically.
