🧬 How Behavior Analysis — Live Profile Works
📍 Primary Risk Indicator
Updates in real-time as you change key fields. For Bank: tracks credit score quality. For Telecom: tracks contract type (Month-to-Month = highest risk). For HR: detects overtime burden.
⏱ Tenure Risk
Short customer relationships churn 3× more. Customers in their first 6 months (Telecom/Bank) or first 2 years at a company (HR) are flagged as HIGH risk automatically.
🔗 Engagement Score
Tracks active engagement signals — number of services subscribed (Telecom), account activity (Bank), or job satisfaction level (HR). Low engagement = likely to leave.
⚡ Why Live?
These indicators update instantly as you type — before you even submit the form. This gives you an early signal of whether the customer is high risk so you can adjust what data you're entering or flag them for retention before running the ML model.
✅ After submitting, the full ML model runs a deep behavior analysis and generates specific Why They Will Churn reasons and How to Prevent It action steps on the result page.
